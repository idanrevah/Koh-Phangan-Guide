Koh Phangan is an island in the Gulf of Thailand in southeast Thailand in Surat Thani Province.
Koh Phangan is best known for its Full Moon Party at Haad Rin Beach.
Koh Phangan has two sister islands: the larger Ko Samui to the south and the smaller Ko Tao to the north.

Estimated perimeter: 40 km (estimated 10 hr average walking time)
From mainland: about 55 km
From Ko Samui: about 15 km
From Ko Tao: about 35 km
Main town: Thong Sala
